My Mentors

My Father- My father has been and continues to be my greatest source of inspiration. Somebody I look upto from celebrating my smallest of achievements to lifting me up from my greatest of failures. He has always been behind me concrete as a rock which help me take my decisions much more freely.

My Mother - Describing her in words is just beyond my capabilities. She is just an epitome of dedication hardwork and truthfullness towards work. She is much more expressive and fun loving but always have managed to adjust her schedule according to my work and study hours.

I still remember his caring gestures after I was at rock bottom after Class X results constantly reminding me that I deserved more than this. He has always been the guide telling what is coming ahead I just got to know what studies would be like two years from now just by seeing him his guidance always helped me.
